Pretty in Pink

BY: HANA | DATE: 4.10.17

I love blazers - they're sleek, they're chic. They instantly add an air of sophistication to any look. Over the years, I've amassed a sizable collection of blazers, and my newest addition has been the pink blazer. I've always fallen more for darker colors, and this light pink addition was definitely a step outside of my comfort zone. But, I've absolutely loved rocking it, and it's definitely become one of my most worn pieces in my wardrobe. I thought I'd share with you some of my favorite outfits with my new blazer.



A failproof way to wear the pink blazer is with an all black outfit underneath. The pink pops against the all black, and the blazer shines as the focal point to your outfit.



Since the pink already makes the outfit more casual, you can also pair it with looser fits too.



The pink also goes well with other pastel colors, such as light blue. Try wearing your pink blazer with light blue jeans and a white t-shirt. It's casual but still put together - great for a brunch date, or for some coffee with your girlfriends.
