SWEATER WEATHER

BY: KELLY | DATE: 4.10.17


I spent my first Spring Break of college in New York City. After finally escaping from snow-covered Ithaca, I dug out my favorite outfit and was ready to explore the city. My lovely photographer friend, Julia, decided to show me around one of her favorite places in the city - the Oculus. This gorgeous mall was just finished this year, and it's located within the World Trade Center. Not only are the stores unique and eyecatching, the architecture in this building is simply breathtaking.



One wardrobe staple that everyone needs is a black sweater. A basic sweater can be dressed up or down in so many ways! You can dress it down with a pair of comfy leggings for a laid back and chill look. Or you can dress it up with some textured pants and fancier shoes. Today, I chose a pair of dark leather pants and black heeled booties for a more dressed up look.



The Oculus is perfect for portrait photography because they have so many interesting backdrops and structures that you can pose in front of. So we went on a spontaneous photoshoot with Julia's film camera around the mall.




We visited Nobletree Coffee, and they had amazing lattes - coming from two coffee enthusiasts, that's a pretty big compliment. The store had a dope ambiance, with nice wooden decorations and little lights sprinkled everywhere.



Fun fact: When you stand in the middle of this circle, aka exactly where I'm sitting in the picture below, and project your voice, you can hear it echoed back to you in waves! That's because this area is built like an auditorium, for speakers and entertainers to perform!



That's it for my first blog post! Thanks for reading it all the way through - hope you enjoyed!
